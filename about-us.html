Our Story

From humble beginnings to culinary excellence

2018

The Beginning

FoodieExpress started as a small family kitchen in Mumbai with a passion for authentic Indian flavors. Our founder, Chef Rajesh, began with just three recipes passed down from his grandmother.

2019

First Restaurant

With growing popularity, we opened our first restaurant in Bandra, Mumbai. Our focus on traditional cooking methods and fresh ingredients quickly made us a local favorite.

2020

Digital Expansion

During the pandemic, we pivoted to delivery-only service. Our app launch connected us with thousands of food lovers across Mumbai, delivering authentic meals safely to their homes.

2022

National Recognition

FoodieExpress won the "Best Indian Food Delivery" award at the National Culinary Awards. We expanded to 5 major cities across India and launched our signature spice blends.

2023

Culinary Innovation

We opened our state-of-the-art culinary center in Delhi, where our chefs experiment with traditional recipes while maintaining authentic flavors. Our menu now features over 200 dishes.

Our Core Values

Principles that guide everything we do

Fresh Ingredients

We source locally and seasonally, ensuring peak freshness and supporting local farmers. No artificial preservatives ever.

Authentic Recipes

Traditional cooking methods passed through generations. We honor the heritage of Indian culinary arts.

Passionate Chefs

Our culinary team pours their heart into every dish, creating memorable dining experiences for our customers.

Sustainable Practices

Eco-friendly packaging, zero food waste initiatives, and energy-efficient kitchens are our commitment to the planet.

Community Focus

We support local communities through food donation programs and culinary training for underprivileged youth.

Innovation

While respecting tradition, we constantly explore new techniques and flavor combinations to delight our customers.
